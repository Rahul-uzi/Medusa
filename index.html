What is the dress code at Medusa?

We maintain a smart casual to formal dress code to preserve the elegant atmosphere of our dining rooms. Sportswear, sandals, and sleeveless shirts are discouraged.

Do I need to reserve a table in advance?

While walk-ins are welcome if space allows, we highly recommend booking a table via our online system to ensure availability, especially for weekend dinner reservations.
