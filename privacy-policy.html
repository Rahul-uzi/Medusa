Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

At La-Medusaa, we collect personal information to provide you with our premium dining, reservation, and delivery services. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Personal Identifiers: Your name, email address, phone number, and physical billing/delivery addresses.
  • Reservation Details: Dates and times of your reservations, party sizes, special dining preferences, dietary restrictions, and occasion notes.
  • Billing & Transaction Info: Payment transaction status, invoice histories, and order records.
  • Device & Usage Data: IP addresses, browser types, operating systems, and usage statistics gathered from your interactions with our website.

2. How We Use Information

We process your personal information with absolute care, utilizing it strictly for the following purposes:

  • To confirm, manage, and verify your table bookings and order placements.
  • To process online orders, deliver meals, and issue purchase receipts.
  • To communicate booking updates, offer customer support, and alert you about orders.
  • To customize and improve your dining experiences by remembering your culinary choices and dietary preferences.
  • To identify and prevent fraudulent transactions, protecting the safety of all our guests.

3. Cookies & Tracking

La-Medusaa uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user navigation and optimize website performance. Cookies help us analyze traffic patterns, remember your language preferences, and keep you securely logged into your account.

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser settings; however, doing so may limit your ability to use certain features on our website, such as managing your shopping cart or making table reservations.

4. Payment Security

We are fully committed to protecting your financial transactions. La-Medusaa utilizes industry-standard Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption to secure all payment pathways.

All online credit card and digital wallet payments are handled through PCI-DSS compliant third-party gateways. We do not store, process, or retain any raw credit card numbers or highly sensitive financial credentials on our local servers.

5. Third Party Services

We may share specific personal information with trusted external partners to perform necessary operational services, including:

  • Payment Processors: To complete transactions securely.
  • Delivery and Logistics Partners: To route and deliver your food orders safely to your location.
  • Reservation and Booking Systems: To coordinate seat assignments and waitlists.
  • Web Analytics Services: To monitor and analyze website metrics for performance improvements.

All third-party vendors are legally bound to protect your data and are strictly prohibited from using your personal information for any secondary purposes.

6. Data Retention

We store your personal data only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the services outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with local accounting/regulatory obligations, or resolve active legal disputes.

When personal information is no longer needed, it is either securely anonymized or permanently deleted from our databases.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have several rights regarding your personal information, which include:

  • The right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to ask us to correct incorrect, incomplete, or outdated information.
  • The right to request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal overrides.
  • The right to withdraw consent for direct marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please submit a request to our management using the contact details provided below.
